Spread cookies


steps :-

  1. preheat kitchen appliance to 3500(1800C ).
  2. in a massive bowl, combine along the spread, sugar, and egg.
  3. scoop out of a spoon of dough and roll it into a ball. place the cookie balls onto a slippy baking sheet
  4. bake for 8- 10 muinutes or till rock bottom of the cookies square measure golden brown
  5. remove from baking sheet and freeze it
  6. Enjoy
